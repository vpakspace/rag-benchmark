## Methodology

**Documents**: 2 technical texts (EN + RU), 8-15 pages each.

**Questions**: 60 bilingual questions (30 per document), 5 types:
simple, relation, multi_hop, global, temporal.

**Evaluation**: 3-level judge (embedding similarity -> keyword overlap -> LLM judge).

**Metrics**: accuracy, avg_confidence, avg_latency.
